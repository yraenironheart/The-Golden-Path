Electric violinist Xani Kolac uses live looping technology and electronic effects in the form of electric guitar pedals, using her feet and toes to manipulate the devices while singing and playing her songs.

Currently, Xani Kolac is writing and performing new songs for The Twoks at the prestigious and inspiring Banff Centre in Canada. You can expect to hear about a brand new collection of recordings in the form of an EP in June.
